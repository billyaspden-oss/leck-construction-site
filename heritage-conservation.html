Heritage &
Conservation

Working closely with English Heritage on the conservation, repair and restoration of historic buildings, listed structures and ecclesiastical buildings across Cumbria — where quality and fine craftsmanship are never compromised.

What We Deliver

Restoring the Past.
With Pride and Precision.

Heritage and conservation work demands a different kind of contractor — one with patience, fine craftsmanship, deep respect for history and the technical skills to work sensitively with historic fabric. Leck Construction has worked closely with English Heritage for many years, delivering conservation repairs and restorations that protect our region's most treasured buildings for future generations.

Our heritage portfolio spans Grade I and Grade II listed buildings, ecclesiastical structures, Victorian parks, historic halls and extraordinary projects in remote locations. Current work includes the ongoing restoration of the 300+ year old Grade II listed Ship Inn on Piel Island — one of Britain's most remarkable heritage projects, accessible only by boat. Every project, large or small, receives the same commitment to quality and detail.

Selected Heritage & Conservation Work

Listed Building — Ongoing
The Ship Inn, Piel Island
Piel Island, Cumbria

Ongoing restoration of the 300+ year old Grade II listed Ship Inn — an extraordinary project on an island accessible only by boat, demanding specialist heritage skills, meticulous logistics and a deep respect for this nationally unique site.

Victorian Park Restoration
Barrow Park
Barrow-in-Furness

Comprehensive restoration of Barrow's Grade II listed Victorian park — including the lake, footpaths, cenotaph and full reinstatement of the historic bandstand.

Ecclesiastical Restoration
St James Church Bell Tower
Barrow-in-Furness

Specialist restoration of the bell tower and bells at St James Church — delicate structural and masonry work carried out to conservation standards.

Historic Hall
Victoria Hall
Barrow-in-Furness

Careful refurbishment of the historic Victoria Hall — preserving an important piece of Barrow's civic heritage while adapting the building for continued community use.

High-Level Restoration
Manjushri Centre
Ulverston, Cumbria

Specialist high-level refurbishment works at the internationally recognised Manjushri Kadampa Meditation Centre — a project requiring both technical precision and cultural sensitivity.

Historic Restoration
Lantern House
Ulverston, Cumbria

Restoration works at Lantern House, Ulverston — sympathetic repair and conservation of a historic building in the heart of this Cumbrian market town.

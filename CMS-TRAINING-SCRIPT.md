# Leck CMS — Training Video Script (~5 min)

A Loom/screen-recording script for Billy to record **for Leck's team**. Narration in
plain English; `[SHOW]` = what's on screen. Aim ~5 minutes. Have one news post and one
vacancy half-prepared so you're not typing from scratch on camera.

---

### 0:00 — Welcome (20s)
> "Hi — this is a quick walkthrough of how to add and update your own job vacancies and
> news on the Leck website. It takes about a minute per item, there's nothing technical,
> and anything you publish appears on the site within a minute or so. Let's jump in."

`[SHOW]` the live site `/news` page, then the editor login screen.

### 0:20 — Logging in (20s)
> "You'll get an email invite from me. Click the link, set a password, and you'll land
> here. Bookmark this page — it's where you'll always come to make changes."

`[SHOW]` log in at the `…sanity.studio` URL. Point out the two things on the left:
**Vacancy** and **News / Newsletter**.

### 0:40 — Add a news post (1m 45s)
> "Let's post a piece of news. Click **News / Newsletter**, then the pencil/plus to
> create a new one."

`[SHOW]` create new. Walk through each field slowly:
- **Headline** — "Type your headline as you'd want it read on the site."
- **Web address** — "Click **Generate** and it fills this in automatically from the
  headline. You can ignore it otherwise."
- **Category** — "Pick the closest one from the list."
- **Date** — "The date of the news."
- **Cover image** — "Drag a photo in here, or click to upload. This is the picture that
  shows on the news page and at the top of the article. Add a short description in the
  image box so Google and screen readers know what it is."
- **Short summary** — "One or two sentences — this is what shows in the news list and on
  Google."
- **Article** — "This is the full story. Type like a normal document. Use the toolbar
  for **bold**, headings, links and bullet points. You can drop images in here too."

> "When you're happy, leave **Published** switched on, and hit **Publish** in the bottom
> right."

`[SHOW]` click **Publish**.

### 2:25 — See it live (30s)
> "Give it about a minute, then refresh the news page on the site… and there it is.
> Click in and you've got a proper article page with your photo and text."

`[SHOW]` refresh `/news`, click the new post → the article page.

### 2:55 — Add a vacancy (1m 30s)
> "Vacancies work the same way. Click **Vacancy**, create a new one."

`[SHOW]` walk the fields:
- **Job title**, **Location**, **Type** (full-time, part-time, etc.), **Salary**
  ("optional — leave blank to hide it").
- **Short summary** — "shown in the vacancies list."
- **Full description** — "the full advert; same toolbar as before."
- **Closing date** — "optional, but handy: after this date the role hides itself
  automatically, so you won't forget to take it down."
- **Apply by email** — "where applications go; already set to admin@leck.co."
- **Publish.**

> "Refresh the careers page and the role's there, with an Apply button. These also feed
> into Google's job listings, so people searching for work locally can find them."

### 4:25 — Editing & taking things down (30s)
> "To change something, open it, edit, and hit **Publish** again. To take a role or a
> story off the site **without deleting it**, just switch **Published** off and publish —
> it disappears from the site but stays here if you want it back later. Only use the
> delete bin if you're sure you won't need it again."

### 4:55 — Wrap (15s)
> "That's it. Vacancies and news are yours to manage whenever you like. Anything bigger —
> new pages, design changes — just drop me a line and I'll handle it. Thanks!"

---

**Tips for recording**
- Pre-write one news post + one vacancy so you're editing, not composing, on camera.
- Keep the live site open in a second tab to show the before/after.
- If a published change isn't showing, wait ~60s and hard-refresh (it's CDN-cached briefly).

What We Deliver

Quality Housing.
Built to Last.

Leck Construction has produced high-quality housing for private clients, housing associations and public sector bodies for over 70 years. As an NHBC Grade A-rated housebuilder — the highest rating available for a small-to-medium builder — our commitment to standards is independently recognised at a national level.

Our private residential portfolio spans bespoke Lake District homes complete with swimming pools, wine cellars, fitness suites and cinema rooms, through to traditional family housing and affordable social homes delivered in partnership with housing associations and local authorities. We offer a complete design-and-build service, taking sole responsibility from initial concept to final handover.

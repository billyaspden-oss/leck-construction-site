Leck Construction is proud to have formed long-term building partnerships with several of Britain's most successful and well-known blue-chip companies. These are not one-off contracts — they are ongoing relationships built on consistent performance, specialist capability and mutual trust over many years.

Our partnership contracts cover the full range of construction activity: from day-to-day maintenance and refurbishment through to major civil engineering works and multi-million pound capital projects. Working within complex, regulated environments — including nuclear-licensed facilities — demands the highest standards of safety, reliability and technical expertise. We deliver all three.

Our Partners

Long-Term Partnership Contracts

Pharmaceutical — Since 1997
GlaxoSmithKline
Ulverston, Cumbria

Following over 40 years of project-based work with Glaxo Chemicals, Leck Construction became the official Civil Engineering and Building Partner in 1997. We maintain a continual presence on the Ulverston site, carrying out maintenance, refurbishment and major civil engineering and building works across one of the world's leading pharmaceutical manufacturing campuses.

Defence — Since 2006
BAE Systems
Barrow-in-Furness, Cumbria

Leck Construction became a Measured Term construction partner to BAE Systems in 2006. We perform maintenance, refurbishment and new-build projects across the Barrow shipyard — one of Britain's most strategically important defence sites. Our experience working within nuclear-licensed environments has enabled us to successfully execute major projects with multi-million pound contract values.

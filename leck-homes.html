What We Deliver

Homes Built for
the Communities We Serve.

Leck Homes is the development arm of Leck Construction Ltd — bringing the same quality and craftsmanship from our contracting work directly to the homes we build and sell. With over 70 years of construction experience behind every project, our developments are designed to complement their surroundings and meet genuine local demand.

From modest family homes to executive residences, we leverage our deep knowledge of South Cumbria to create developments in areas offering real lifestyle benefits. Every Leck Homes development is built by us, for the community — with character, care and lasting quality at its core.

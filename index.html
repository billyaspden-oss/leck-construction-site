Our Story

Eight Decades of
Craftsmanship

Founded in 1946 as John Leck & Son, Leck Construction Ltd is one of the most experienced and respected construction companies in South Lakeland. With our head office at Leck House, Barrow-in-Furness, and a regional presence stretching from the Lake District National Park to Manchester, we deliver across every scale of project.

Our experienced management team and skilled local workforce span groundwork, bricklaying, joinery, civil engineering and heritage conservation. We offer a full design-and-build service — taking sole responsibility from initial concept through to final completion, for clients ranging from private homeowners to major corporate partners including GlaxoSmithKline and BAE Systems.

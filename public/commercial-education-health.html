What We Deliver

Buildings That Serve
Communities.

Leck Construction has a proud history of delivering landmark public and commercial buildings across South Cumbria. Iconic projects including Emlyn Hughes House and Barrow Magistrates Court stand as testament to our capability in complex, high-profile construction — delivering on time, on budget and to an uncompromising standard.

Our commercial, education and health portfolio spans NHS healthcare facilities, private nursing homes, arts centres, sports halls, restaurant and hospitality builds, and office developments. We work closely with local authorities, NHS trusts, Cumbria County Council and private clients to deliver buildings that genuinely serve their communities.

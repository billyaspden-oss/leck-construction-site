Heritage &
Conservation

Decades of conservation, repair and restoration work on listed buildings, ecclesiastical structures and historic landmarks across Cumbria. We've worked alongside English Heritage and other custodians of the region's most treasured buildings — and we welcome new heritage enquiries.

Our Heritage Track Record

Restoring the Past.
With Pride and Precision.

Heritage and conservation work demands a different kind of contractor — one with patience, fine craftsmanship, deep respect for history and the technical skills to work sensitively with historic fabric. Over many years Leck Construction has worked alongside English Heritage and other heritage custodians, delivering conservation repairs and restorations on some of the region's most important buildings.

Our heritage portfolio spans Grade I and Grade II listed buildings, ecclesiastical structures, Victorian parks, historic halls and extraordinary projects in remote locations — including the 300+ year old Grade II listed Ship Inn on Piel Island (accessible only by boat), Barrow's Grade II listed Victorian park and the bell tower of St James's Church. We don't take on heritage work continuously, but the skills, suppliers and approach are retained — and we'd be glad to discuss any new project.

Selected Heritage & Conservation Work

Listed Building — Ongoing
The Ship Inn, Piel Island
Piel Island, Cumbria

Ongoing restoration of the 300+ year old Grade II listed Ship Inn — an extraordinary project on an island accessible only by boat, demanding specialist heritage skills, meticulous logistics and a deep respect for this nationally unique site.

Victorian Park Restoration
Barrow Park
Barrow-in-Furness

Comprehensive restoration of Barrow's Grade II listed Victorian park — including the lake, footpaths, cenotaph and full reinstatement of the historic bandstand.

Ecclesiastical Restoration
St James Church Bell Tower
Barrow-in-Furness

Specialist restoration of the bell tower and bells at St James Church — delicate structural and masonry work carried out to conservation standards.

Historic Hall
Victoria Hall
Barrow-in-Furness

Careful refurbishment of the historic Victoria Hall — preserving an important piece of Barrow's civic heritage while adapting the building for continued community use.

High-Level Restoration
Manjushri Centre
Ulverston, Cumbria

Specialist high-level refurbishment works at the internationally recognised Manjushri Kadampa Meditation Centre — a project requiring both technical precision and cultural sensitivity.

Historic Restoration
Lantern House
Ulverston, Cumbria

Restoration works at Lantern House, Ulverston — sympathetic repair and conservation of a historic building in the heart of this Cumbrian market town.

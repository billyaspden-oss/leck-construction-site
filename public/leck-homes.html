About Leck Homes

The residential arm of Leck Construction.

Since 1946 we've been building across South Cumbria — schools, hospitals, civic landmarks, industrial facilities and thousands of homes. Leck Homes is our own residential development brand. Every house we build under the Leck Homes name is constructed by the same team, to the same standards, as the work we do for our largest commercial clients.

What that means for you: an NHBC Grade A registered housebuilder, materials and labour sourced locally where possible, and a contractor that's been here for nearly 80 years — and intends to be here for the next 80.

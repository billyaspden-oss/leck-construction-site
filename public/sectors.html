What

From long-term strategic partnerships and commercial construction to civil engineering, residential development and heritage conservation — six sectors delivered across South Cumbria and the North West.

Our Capabilities

Six Areas of Expertise

Eight decades of building in one region has given us deep specialism across six distinct sectors. Each has its own team, its own clients and its own track record — but all share the same approach: thorough planning, consistent delivery and accountability to a community we'll still be working in a generation from now.

01 · Strategic Partnerships

Strategic Partnerships

Long-term measured term contracts delivering maintenance, refurbishment and major capital works for some of the region's largest industrial clients.

  • GlaxoSmithKline — partner since 1997
  • BAE Systems — partner since 2006
  • Nuclear-licensed environments
  • Multi-million pound contract values

Heritage & Conservation
April 2026

Ship Inn Restoration Complete on Piel Island

Leck Construction has successfully completed the restoration of the Grade II listed Ship Inn on Piel Island — one of Cumbria's most distinctive heritage landmarks. Work included structural masonry repairs, lime pointing and full internal refurbishment.

Commercial & Health
March 2026

New NHS Healthcare Contract Awarded in Ulverston

We are proud to announce the award of a new contract to deliver a primary care facility in Ulverston for NHS Cumbria. The scheme will provide expanded GP services to over 12,000 patients across the Furness Peninsula.

Company News
February 2026

ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Certification Successfully Renewed

Following an independent audit by NQA, Leck Construction has renewed its ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management certification — reinforcing our commitment to reducing environmental impact across all operations and project sites.

Commercial
January 2026

Wings Nightclub Refurbishment Completed on Programme

Our commercial team completed a full internal refurbishment of the Wings venue in Barrow-in-Furness, delivering the project on time and within budget ahead of the client's planned reopening date.

Major Industrial Contract Secured at Lillyhall Business Park

Leck Construction has been appointed as principal contractor for a new portal frame industrial unit at Lillyhall Business Park in West Cumbria, adding to our growing portfolio of industrial and civil engineering projects across the region.

Private & Social Housing

Affordable Housing Scheme Handed Over to Cumbria County Council

We are pleased to have handed over a 14-unit affordable housing scheme in Barrow-in-Furness to Cumbria County Council, delivering high-quality homes for local families as part of our ongoing social housing programme.

BAE Systems Measured Term Contract Renewed for Further Three Years

We are delighted to announce that our long-standing measured term contract with BAE Systems has been renewed for a further three-year term — a testament to the quality of our work and the strength of a relationship built over nearly two decades.

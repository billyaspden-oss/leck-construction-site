Industrial & Civil
Engineering

Portal frame buildings, reinforced concrete structures, water treatment, bunding, drainage and road contracts — heavy civil and industrial expertise deployed across Cumbria and beyond.

What We Deliver

Heavy Civil &
Industrial Expertise.

Leck Construction has a long track record delivering complex civil engineering and industrial construction contracts across the region. From large-scale portal frame buildings and industrial facilities to specialist civil works including reinforced concrete, bunding, water treatment and piling — we have the capability, plant and experience to deliver.

Our industrial and civil engineering work spans sectors from pharmaceutical and defence through to retail and leisure. Whether it's a new workshop and offices, a drive-through facility or a specialist pump house serving a major industrial site, we approach every contract with the same rigour and commitment to quality.

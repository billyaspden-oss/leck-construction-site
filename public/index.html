About Leck Construction

Excellence in Construction Across South Cumbria

Family-owned and operated from Barrow-in-Furness since 1946, Leck Construction is one of South Cumbria's most established contractors. Our client list includes GlaxoSmithKline, BAE Systems, the NHS and Cumbria County Council — from single bespoke homes to multi-million pound commercial and industrial contracts.

To be the most trusted commercial contractor in South Cumbria — delivering quality buildings on programme, within budget and to the highest standards of safety and sustainability. We are committed to investing in our people, our communities and our environment.

  • Quality and craftsmanship in everything we build
  • Long-term relationships with clients and supply chain
  • Safe sites, every day, on every project
  • Reinvesting in Cumbrian communities

Our standards are independently verified at a national level across quality, safety and environmental management.

  • NHBC — Grade A Rated Housebuilder
  • ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management
  • ISO 14001:2015 — Environmental Management
  • CHAS — Accredited Contractor
  • NFB — National Federation of Builders
  • Constructionline — Gold Member

As holders of ISO 14001:2015, environmental responsibility is embedded across every project we deliver. From waste reduction on site to responsible sourcing of materials, we work to minimise our impact on the Lake District and Cumbrian landscapes we operate within.

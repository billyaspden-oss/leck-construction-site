How We Operate

Our
Approach.

Eight decades of construction have taught us that quality, safety and trust are not optional extras — they are the foundation of everything we do. This page sets out how we operate and what we stand for.

Vision & Values

Built on Integrity.
Delivered with Quality.

Leck Construction was founded in 1946 on a simple principle: do good work, treat people fairly, and stand behind everything you build. That hasn't changed.

We are a family-owned business, and that means something. Decisions are made here, not in a boardroom in London. When a client calls, they speak to someone who knows their project. When something goes wrong — as it occasionally does on any construction site — we fix it. No passing the buck, no hiding behind contract small print.

Our values are straightforward: quality over shortcuts, honesty over spin, long-term relationships over single transactions. We measure success not just by profit but by the buildings still standing after 40 years, the clients who keep coming back, and the apprentices who joined us at 16 and are now running sites.

Health & Safety

Everyone Goes Home Safe.

Health and safety is not a compliance exercise. It is a commitment to every person who works on our sites — directly employed or subcontracted — and to the communities around them.

  • All Site Managers hold SMSTS (Site Management Safety Training Scheme) certification
  • Operatives hold current CSCS cards appropriate to their trade and level
  • Regular toolbox talks and site briefings on every active project
  • Robust Method Statement and Risk Assessment process for every task
  • First Aid-trained personnel on every site at all times
  • Incident reporting and near-miss culture — we investigate, learn and improve
  • CHAS accredited — verified by an independent health and safety assessment body
Environment & Sustainability

Building Responsibly
for Future Generations.

We hold ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management certification — independently audited and renewed annually. It is the framework that keeps our environmental commitments concrete and measurable.

In practice, this means waste management plans on every site, responsible sourcing of materials, minimising dust, noise and disruption in residential areas, and working with subcontractors who share our standards.

We are at an early stage of our sustainability journey and we are honest about that. We are not making net-zero pledges we cannot yet back up. What we are doing is measuring our impact, reducing it where we can, and building sustainability considerations into every project from the design stage.

  • ISO 14001:2015 certified — environmental management audited annually by NQA
  • Site waste management plans on all projects above threshold
  • Preference for locally sourced materials to reduce transport emissions
  • Energy and water consumption monitoring on long-duration sites

Community & Social Value

Rooted in South Cumbria.

We have been part of this community for over 70 years. Our workforce is local, our supply chain is local and the buildings we construct will be here long after we are gone. That carries a responsibility we take seriously.

  • Apprenticeships in bricklaying, joinery, site management and surveying — in partnership with Furness College
  • Preference for local subcontractors and suppliers wherever the programme and budget allow
  • Support for community projects and local charities across Barrow and the Furness Peninsula
  • Employment and skills development at all levels — from school leaver to senior professional
  • Engagement with Cumbria County Council on affordable housing and social infrastructure

Location

Walney Island, South Cumbria.

A coastal community on the western edge of Barrow-in-Furness, connected to the mainland by the Jubilee Bridge. Walney is home to two nationally significant nature reserves, a thriving primary school and easy access to the Lake District beyond.

Avon Wood sits on the south side of Walney, a five-minute walk from the channel and ten minutes from South Walney Nature Reserve. The village school is on the same road, the BAE Systems Barrow shipyard is fifteen minutes by car across the bridge, and the M6 is forty minutes away via the A590.

For weekends, the Lake District National Park boundary is forty minutes by car — Coniston, Hawkshead and Windermere are all under an hour.
